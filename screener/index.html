Clinical Test Screener

Designed as a digital questionnaire, this application serves the critical function of identifying prospective candidates for clinical trials. It boasts a versatile array of question methodologies, customizable to accommodate diverse client specifications and requirements. Through its intuitive interface and robust backend architecture, this platform enables efficient screening of potential patients, ensuring comprehensive data collection while adhering to stringent regulatory standards and protocols. Leveraging cutting-edge technologies, it offers flexibility and scalability, empowering healthcare providers and research organizations to streamline the patient recruitment process and accelerate the advancement of medical science.
